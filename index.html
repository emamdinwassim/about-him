Wassim Emamdin is the Managing Director and CEO of Super Deals Mauritius Co Ltd — a government-approved online reservation agency that has become one of Mauritius's most trusted names in property rentals.

Before founding his companies, Wassim built deep expertise as a marketing manager across multiple hotels in Mauritius, giving him a unique understanding of hospitality, client expectations, and what truly makes a property stand out.
